Rowing on the Occoquan

The Occoquan reservoir is formed by a dam on the Occ. River, which Bull Run flows into
There are 4 rowing sites on the river/reservoir.
Lake Ridge Marina is a Prince William County parks facility, home to club rowing and hs teams. sandy Run Regional Park is in Fairfax Station and many teams & clubs row there.
Bull Run Marina is a smaller facility near Clifton, VA.There is also a small rowing site below the dam in Occoquan Regl Park.

Rowing on the Anacostia

There are 2 locations to row on the Anacostia River. One is Capital Rowing Club in SW DC near Nationals Park.
The other is Bladensburg Park in PG County Maryland.
Several teams & clubs use each location.
